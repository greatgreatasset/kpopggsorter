    Keyboard Controls

    Keyboard controls during sorting:
    H/LeftArrow (pick left)
    J/DownArrow (undo)
    K/UpArrow (tie)
    L/RightArrow (pick right)
    S (save progress).

    Before sorting:
    S/Enter (start sorting)
    L (load progress).

    1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
